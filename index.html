MindSpark is the annual, national level technical festival of College of Engineering, Pune. Over a span of 15 years, MindSpark has grown to become one of the biggest technical college fests in the country.


THIS YEAR'S THEME

Our great nation has had some of the greatest minds in the world to lead to our country's rapid growth in science and technology. This year's MINDSPARK theme, is inspired by a very dynamic personality who has also been part of COEP's previous Satellite projects. Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam
